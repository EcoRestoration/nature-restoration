Across Europe, peatlands are being restored to bring back their vital role as carbon stores, water regulators, and biodiversity hotspots. This interactive map highlights ongoing and finished restoration projects, showing where action is taking place and how landscapes are being reconnected with their natural functions. Click on any location to explore individual projects, learn about their goals, and discover some restoration results, the people and partnerships behind them. Together, these efforts form a growing network of restoration sites—each contributing to a more resilient and climate-stable future.
